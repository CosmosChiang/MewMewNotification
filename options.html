About

MewMewNotification

MewMewNotification - Chrome 擴充功能，用於更高效、穩定地接收與管理 Redmine 通知。

Features:

  • Efficient Redmine notification checks
  • Desktop notifications with sound alerts
  • Unread, read, and all notification inbox views
  • Mark notifications as read and clear history
  • Direct links and quick issue actions
  • Customizable check intervals and quiet hours
  • Project and change-type notification filters
  • Assigned and watched issue support
  • Multi-language support (EN/ZH_TW/ZH_CN/JA)
  • Batched storage writes and bounded retry handling
  • Bounded cache and optimized notification rendering
